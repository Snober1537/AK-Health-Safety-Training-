About AK Health & Safety Training

At AK Health & Safety Training, we are committed to providing world-class training and certifications in occupational health and safety. Our programs are designed to meet international standards and prepare professionals for safer workplaces across industries.

Our Certifications

  • NEBOSH: International General Certificate (IGC) and Level 6 Diploma.
  • IOSH: IOSH Managing Safely.
  • OSHA: 30-hour training programs for the American context.
  • OTHM: Level 6 Diploma in Occupational Health and Safety.

Specialized Courses

  • Risk Assessment: Hazard identification and risk assessment.
  • First Aid: Immediate incident response training.
  • Fire Safety: Fire risk assessment and prevention.
  • ISO Standards: Lead auditor courses for ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001.
  • Industry-Specific Training: Construction safety, manual handling, electrical safety.

Whether you are an individual seeking professional growth or an organization aiming to strengthen workplace safety, AK Health & Safety Training provides the knowledge and skills you need to succeed.
